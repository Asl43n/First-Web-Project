Here is the instruction to become the Employe of the Month

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • half tsp salt

  • ground beef
  • cup bread crumbs

Steps

  1. preheat grill
  2. Whisk egg, salt and pepper
  3. add ground beef and crumbs
  4. form into patties
  5. cook til good
